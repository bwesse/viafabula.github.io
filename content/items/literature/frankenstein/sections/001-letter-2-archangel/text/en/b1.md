Letter 2: Archangel

This adaptation keeps the central events, motives, and consequences.

Walton reaches Archangel and hires a ship and crew. The sailors seem brave and dependable.

He praises his gentle lieutenant and the generous shipmaster, who once gave up a marriage so another man could be happy.

Still, Walton has no equal with whom he can share his deepest hopes. He wants a friend who will understand and correct him.

The result shapes the choices that follow.
