Chapter 10: Meeting on the Glacier

In contemporary prose, the section develops as follows.

On the glacier at Montanvert, the creature approaches Victor with astonishing speed. Victor curses it as a murderer.

The creature speaks clearly and asks Victor to listen before judging. It says that misery made it violent and that its creator owes it compassion.

Victor reluctantly agrees. They enter a hut, where the creature begins the story of its lonely life.

What happens here changes the direction of everything that follows.
