Chapter 17: A Companion

In contemporary prose, the section develops as follows.

The creature argues that loneliness, not natural evil, made it violent. It asks Victor to create a female being like itself.

The pair will leave Europe forever and live far from humans. The creature promises peace if Victor grants this one chance of companionship.

Victor fears the consequences but recognizes his duty as creator. He agrees, and the creature disappears to wait.

What happens here changes the direction of everything that follows.
