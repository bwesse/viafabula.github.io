Chapter 23: The Wedding Night

This adaptation keeps the central events, motives, and consequences.

Victor and Elizabeth marry and travel to an inn by the lake. Victor searches for the creature while Elizabeth waits alone.

A scream reveals the true revenge: Elizabeth has been murdered. Victor sees the creature grinning outside and fires, but it escapes.

Alphonse dies from grief soon afterward. Victor tells a magistrate everything, but when official help fails, he swears to hunt the creature himself.

The result shapes the choices that follow.
