Chapter 11: The Creature Awakens

This version tells the main events in a connected way.

The creature remembers waking with confused senses. Light, darkness, hunger, cold, and fire are all new to it.

People scream or attack when they see its face. It learns that human society judges its appearance before its actions.

Seeking shelter, it hides beside a cottage occupied by a poor family. From there it begins to observe human life.

These events prepare the next part of the book.
