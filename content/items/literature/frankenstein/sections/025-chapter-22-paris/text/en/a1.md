Chapter 22: Elizabeth’s Letter

This is the essential story in clear language.

In Paris, Victor receives a letter from Elizabeth. She asks whether he loves someone else and offers to release him from their engagement.

Victor answers that he loves her, but still hides his secret. He believes the creature plans to kill him on the wedding night.

He accepts the marriage and prepares weapons for his own defense. He does not understand that the threat may be aimed at Elizabeth.
