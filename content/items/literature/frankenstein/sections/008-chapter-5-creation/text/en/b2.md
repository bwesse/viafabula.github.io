Chapter 5: The Creation

This fuller adaptation preserves the section’s central action and ideas.

On a dark November night, Victor brings the creature to life. Its yellow eyes open, but the result horrifies him.

Victor runs away and dreams that Elizabeth turns into his dead mother. When he wakes, the creature stands beside his bed and reaches toward him.

Henry Clerval arrives in Ingolstadt, but Victor collapses into a long fever. The abandoned creature disappears into the city.

The section therefore links personal choice with its wider consequences.
