Letter 3: Progress

In contemporary prose, the section develops as follows.

Walton sends a short letter from the sea. The voyage has begun well, and the crew remains confident.

Although ice floats around the ship, Walton says he will be careful. He promises Margaret that he will not take foolish risks.

His words sound calm, but his desire for glory remains clear. He believes determination will carry him through every danger.

What happens here changes the direction of everything that follows.
