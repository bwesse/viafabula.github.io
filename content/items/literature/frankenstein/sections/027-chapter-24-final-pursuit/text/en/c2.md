Chapter 24: The Final Pursuit

At this highly advanced level, the section is framed through its precise narrative and thematic consequences.

Victor pursues the creature across Europe and into the Arctic. His enemy leaves food and messages that keep the chase alive.

After Victor finishes his story aboard Walton’s ship, the crew demands to return south. Walton gives up his ambition to save their lives, and Victor dies soon afterward.

The creature appears beside Victor’s body. It mourns its crimes and loneliness, promises to die on a funeral fire, and disappears into the darkness.

The episode consequently functions as both a narrative turning point and a concentrated statement of the work’s larger concerns.
