Chapter 6: Clerval and Elizabeth’s Letter

This adaptation keeps the central events, motives, and consequences.

Henry patiently nurses Victor back to health. A letter from Elizabeth brings news of home and of the kind servant Justine Moritz.

Victor avoids science because it reminds him of the creature. Henry studies languages, and the two friends enjoy literature and the countryside.

Spring restores Victor’s strength. He plans to return to Geneva, unaware that tragedy has already reached his family.

The result shapes the choices that follow.
