Chapter 2: Childhood Curiosity

Victor, Elizabeth, and Henry Clerval become close friends. Elizabeth loves poetry and nature, while Henry dreams of heroic adventures.

Victor wants to discover the hidden laws of the world. Old writers such as Agrippa and Paracelsus fill him with dreams of forbidden knowledge.

A lightning strike destroys a tree and introduces him to modern electricity. Yet no adult clearly teaches him why the old theories are wrong.
