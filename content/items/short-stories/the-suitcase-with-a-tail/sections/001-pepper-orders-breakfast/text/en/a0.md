Nina opened the bag a few centimeters.

“You are not a paper flower,” Nina whispered.

Nina wanted to call the number on the label, but her phone went dark.

Before Nina could explain more, a breakfast cart came through the carriage.

“Stop that cat!” shouted the waiter.

Pepper went under a seat, over a suitcase, and through the open door of the quiet carriage.

At last, Nina caught Pepper with help from a little girl and half a cheese sandwich.
