The final path crossed an open hill.
The cart could not go over it, and the ground on both sides was soft with rain.

Lea found a narrow space between the tree and a rock.
Inés guided the wheels while they pulled the cart sideways, one careful meter at a time.

The cart became stuck in the mud.
Omar pushed, Inés pulled, and the cart rolled onto the path again.

They reached the lighthouse at 11:32.
Together they carried the battery up, resting after every twelve steps.

At the top, Inés opened the metal box below the lamp.
“It must still be in the tool shed beside the lower gate.”

Omar looked down the long staircase.
Inés stayed to prepare the lamp while Lea and Omar ran back outside.

They followed the lighthouse wall to the left, passed a water tank, and found the lower gate.
Inside, the cable lay on a shelf marked EMERGENCY.
