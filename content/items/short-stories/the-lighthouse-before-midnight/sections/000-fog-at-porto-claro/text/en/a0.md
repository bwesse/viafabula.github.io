Lea and Omar arrived in Porto Claro on the last ferry of the evening.

The journey had been calm, but thick fog now covered the harbor.

“The guesthouse is near the church,” Lea said, reading the message on her phone.

Before they left the harbor, a woman ran out of a small office.

Her name was Inés, and she looked after the harbor lights.

“A fishing boat is returning before midnight,” Inés explained.

Lea looked at Omar.
