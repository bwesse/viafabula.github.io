Sela led them beside the stream.
Riko walked slowly behind her and did not complain.

When the trees ended, the storm moved east.
Sela nodded.

Riko ran up the last hill, but he stopped at every turn and looked back.
In this way, quick feet and a careful map reached the meadow together before dark.

Owl was waiting under an old tree.
“Which of you won?” asked Rabbit, who had followed Owl to the meadow.

Riko looked at Sela.
No one understood the second answer, but Owl laughed.

The animals planted the seeds the next morning.
He still liked speed, but now he also liked arriving.

Later that summer, moonflowers appeared on the meadow.
They were smaller, but they shone just as brightly.
