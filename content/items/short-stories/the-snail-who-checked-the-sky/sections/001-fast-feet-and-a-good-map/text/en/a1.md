Before leaving, Sela wrapped her seed bag in two broad leaves.
She also asked Owl to draw the three paths in the earth.

“You will lose time with all that preparation,” said Rabbit.
“But I will know where I am.”

Riko was already far ahead.
When he passed a group of mice, he ran around them twice, just to show how fast he was.

At the berry bushes, Riko stopped for a snack.
He put the paper seed bag under a bush and told two birds about his great speed.

Sela continued beside the stream.
The path was longer, but the trees protected her from the wind.

Near noon, the air became cold.
“A little rain cannot catch me,” he said.

Thunder answered from the hill.
Sela pulled the leaves more tightly around her bag and continued without stopping.
