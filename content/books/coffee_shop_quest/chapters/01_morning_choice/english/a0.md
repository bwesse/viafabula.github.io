# Coffee Shop Quest — A0

## [scene:morning] Morning
You are Sam. You live in a small town. You are hungry. You want coffee.
Sam thinks, "I can try English today."

- Go to the coffee shop now -> shop
- Stay in bed -> bed

## [scene:shop] The Coffee Shop
Sam walks to the shop. The barista smiles.
The barista says, "Hello! What do you want?"

Useful words: coffee, tea, milk, small, large.

- Say, "A small coffee, please." -> order_coffee
- Say, "A large tea, please." -> order_tea

## [scene:bed] Staying in Bed
Sam stays in bed. Sam feels slow. Sam thinks, "I need to move."

Useful words: tired, slow, bed, go, need.

- Get up now and go to the coffee shop -> shop
- Sleep more -> end_sleep

## [scene:order_coffee] Ordering Coffee
Sam says, "A small coffee, please." The barista nods.
Sam pays. Sam waits. The coffee smells good.

Useful words: small, coffee, pay, wait, good.

- Sit by the window and drink -> sit_listen
- Take the cup and walk in the park -> walk_drink

## [scene:order_tea] Ordering Tea
Sam says, "A large tea, please." The barista fills a cup.
Sam pays. Sam holds a hot cup of tea.

Useful words: large, tea, hot, cup, hold.

- Add milk and sugar -> sit_listen
- Drink plain tea -> walk_drink

## [scene:sit_listen] Sitting and Listening
Sam sits. Sam hears people talk.
One person says, "How are you?" Another says, "I am fine."
Sam repeats quietly. Sam learns.

Useful words: sit, hear, talk, repeat, learn.

- Say "Hello" to a neighbor -> practice
- Just listen more -> quiet_finish

## [scene:walk_drink] Walking with a Drink
Sam walks with the cup. The air is cool.
Sam sees a park bench.

Useful words: walk, drink, park, bench, cool.

- Sit on the bench and read signs -> practice
- Keep walking and finish the drink -> quiet_finish

## [scene:practice] Practice
Sam says, "Hello!" The neighbor smiles.
Sam asks, "How are you?" The neighbor says, "I am good. And you?"
Sam answers, "I am good too."
Sam feels happy. Sam used English today.

## [scene:quiet_finish] Quiet Finish
Sam does not speak, but Sam listens.
Sam reads signs like "Open", "Close", "Thank you".
Sam learns new words.
Sam walks home. Sam feels calm.

## [scene:end_sleep] Sleepy Ending
Sam sleeps again. The day stays quiet.
Sam will try again another day.
