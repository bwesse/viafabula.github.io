# Coffee Shop Quest — A1

## [scene:morning] Wake Up
You are Sam, a beginner English learner. It is Saturday at 8:00.
You are hungry and want coffee. You also want to practice English today.

- Get dressed and go to the coffee shop -> walk_out
- Stay in bed and scroll the phone -> bed

## [scene:walk_out] Walking Out
Sam puts on shoes and a jacket. The air outside is cool.
Sam walks to the small coffee shop on Main Street.

Useful words: jacket, outside, cool, street, shop.

- Open the door and greet the barista -> greet
- Wait outside and read the menu board -> read_menu

## [scene:bed] At Home
Sam lies in bed and checks the phone. The screen is bright.
Sam reads the same words again and feels bored.

Useful words: screen, bright, bored, same, again.

- Put the phone down and go out -> walk_out
- Stay in bed and fall asleep again -> end_sleep

## [scene:greet] Greeting
Sam opens the door. The bell rings. The barista smiles.
Sam says, "Good morning!" The barista says, "Good morning! What can I get you?"

Useful phrases: Good morning, What can I get you, I would like.

- Say, "I would like a small coffee, please." -> order_coffee
- Ask, "Do you have tea with milk?" -> order_tea

## [scene:read_menu] Reading the Menu
Sam waits near the door. The menu board has simple words:
coffee, tea, milk, sugar, small, large.
Sam repeats the words quietly to practice.

- Go inside and order a small coffee -> order_coffee
- Go inside and order a large tea -> order_tea

## [scene:order_coffee] Ordering Coffee
Sam says, "I would like a small coffee, please." The barista nods and makes it.
Sam pays and gets a warm paper cup.

Useful words: would like, please, nod, warm, cup.

- Sit by the window to listen to people -> sit_listen
- Take the cup and walk to the park -> walk_drink

## [scene:order_tea] Ordering Tea
Sam asks, "Do you have tea with milk?" The barista says, "Yes! Small or large?"
Sam answers, "Large, please." The barista makes a large tea with milk.

Useful words: have, with, milk, small, large.

- Add sugar and sit near a group of students -> sit_listen
- Take the tea outside and walk slowly -> walk_drink

## [scene:sit_listen] Listening Practice
Sam sits inside. People talk about their day.
One person says, "How are you?" Another says, "I'm fine, thanks."
Sam repeats the phrases quietly. Sam writes one phrase in a small notebook.

Useful phrases: How are you, I'm fine, thanks, notebook.

- Smile and say "Hello" to a person nearby -> small_talk
- Keep listening and write two new words -> quiet_finish

## [scene:walk_drink] Walking Practice
Sam walks with the drink. Birds sing. Cars pass.
Sam reads signs: "Open", "Slow", "Park". Sam says the words out loud.

Useful words: birds, cars, signs, open, slow.

- Sit on a bench and read a poster -> small_talk
- Finish the drink while walking home -> quiet_finish

## [scene:small_talk] Small Talk
Sam smiles and says, "Hello!" The other person smiles back.
Sam asks, "How are you?" The person says, "I'm good. How are you?"
Sam answers, "I'm good too. I like this coffee."
Sam feels proud. Sam used English with a real person.

## [scene:quiet_finish] Quiet Success
Sam does not talk, but Sam reads and repeats words.
Sam finishes the drink. Sam thinks, "I learned new words today."
Sam walks home. Sam plans to speak next time.

## [scene:end_sleep] Sleepy Ending
Sam sleeps again. No practice today. Tomorrow is another chance.
