# Discovering Klagenfurt

Ben searched for a university.
He wanted to study robotics.
He wanted mountains too.
He wanted a good place to live.

Then he found Klagenfurt.
Klagenfurt is in Austria.
It is a small city.
It has a beautiful lake.
It has mountains nearby.

The university had a good program.
Robotics and Artificial Intelligence.
This was perfect for Ben.

Ben liked what he saw.
Lakes. Mountains. Italy nearby.
Good weather. Nice people.

He made a decision.
He would go to Klagenfurt.
He would study there.
He packed his bags.
He packed his snowboard.

A new adventure was starting.
