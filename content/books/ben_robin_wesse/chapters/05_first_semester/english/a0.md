# First Semester

Ben goes to Klagenfurt.
He has a backpack.
He has a snowboard.

Ben studies art before.
He paints and draws.
He never studies robots.
This is new for him.

He thinks: "I try this."
"If it is bad, I go."
"I can snowboard here."

The classes are hard.
Math is hard.
Physics is hard.
Ben does not understand.

But Ben studies a lot.
He watches videos.
He reads books.
He works hard.

Ben passes his first exam!
He is happy.
He passes more exams.

Ben meets Tim.
Tim is from Hamburg too.
They are friends now.
They study together.

On weekends, Ben goes to the mountains.
He loves snowboarding.
The mountains are beautiful.

Ben likes Klagenfurt.
The university is good.
The mountains are good.
Ben is happy here.
