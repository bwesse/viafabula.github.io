# Prologue

This is the story of Ben Robin Wesse.
Ben is a young man from Hamburg, Germany.
He is 22 years old now.

Ben has always loved adventure.
He likes to explore new places.
He is interested in robots and technology.
He also loves snowboarding in the mountains.

For a long time, Ben did not know what to do with his life.
He was not sure what to study.
He was not sure where to live.
But he was not afraid to try new things.

Ben traveled to many countries.
He went to Canada.
He went to South America.
He found a beautiful place in Austria.
He studied robotics there.

Ben worked on drones with his friend Tim.
They built amazing flying robots.
They won a big competition.
Now Ben lives in Barcelona, Spain.
He studies for his master's degree.

This book tells the story of Ben's journey.
It is a story about following your dreams.
It is about finding your path in life.
Come and discover how Ben found his way.
