# Victory in Alabama

Huntsville, Alabama. The final competition. This was what we had worked so hard for.

We built two new drones: RED and BULL. They were bigger than the London drones. More power. More sensors. I designed them in Fusion 360.

Before leaving Austria, I printed backup parts for everything. Frames, motor mounts, landing gear. If something could break, I had a spare.

This was the best decision I ever made.
