So the wolf huffed and he puffed and he blew the house in.
The straw flew everywhere and the house collapsed in a heap.
The little pig squealed and ran as fast as he could to the second little pig's house of sticks.
"Brother! Brother! Let me in!" he cried. "The wolf blew down my house!"
The second little pig quickly opened the door and let his brother inside.
Just then, they heard a terrible howl as the wolf approached.
